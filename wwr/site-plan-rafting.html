Overview

Purpose

As the proud owner of our rafting business, our website aims to establish a dynamic online presence. We strive to showcase the exhilarating experiences we offer, provide essential information, and attract adventure seekers to join our thrilling rafting trips.

Audience

Our target customers are adventure enthusiasts, thrill-seekers, and nature lovers who are seeking exhilarating and memorable experiences. They typically fall within the age range of 18 to 45, have a moderate to high level of disposable income, and are passionate about outdoor activities.

They want not only an adrenaline rush but also a deep connection with nature through exciting and well-guided rafting adventures. Our audience expects to find detailed information about our rafting trips, safety measures, and the breathtaking locations we explore.

When accessing our website, they are looking for a user-friendly experience that caters to their preferences, whether they are using a desktop, laptop, or mobile device. The website should provide easy navigation, engaging visuals, and compelling content that resonates with their sense of adventure.
